Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Introduction

Backpacker ("we," "our," or "the App") is a mobile application that enables users to combine shipments from online shopping platforms and coordinate group orders with friends. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Apple, we receive:

  • Your name (or a pseudonym if you choose to hide your email)
  • Your email address (or an Apple relay email)
  • A unique user identifier

Contacts

If you grant permission, the App accesses your device's contact list solely to help you find friends who are also using Backpacker. We do not store your full contact list on our servers. We only check email addresses against our user database to identify existing Backpacker users.

Shipment and Order Data

When you use the App, we collect:

  • Shipment room details (titles, platform names, deadlines)
  • Item information you add to rooms (item names, URLs, prices, notes)
  • Order status updates
  • Messages sent within shipment room chats

Device Information

We collect push notification tokens (to send you notifications) and basic device information necessary for app functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your Backpacker account
  • Enable you to create and join shipment rooms
  • Send push notifications about room activity, status changes, and deadlines
  • Help you find and connect with friends on the platform
  • Facilitate in-app messaging between room participants
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services, which include Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Firebase Cloud Storage, and Firebase Cloud Messaging.

All data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS). Your data is stored in servers located in the European Union (europe-west region).

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With other Backpacker users: Your display name and items are visible to participants within shipment rooms you join. Chat messages are visible to room participants.
  • With service providers: We use Google Firebase for backend infrastructure. Google's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the App
  • Correct inaccurate information by updating your profile
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Withdraw consent for contacts access through your device settings
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings

For users in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Data Retention

  • Your account data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Shipment room data is automatically archived 30 days after all items are distributed
  • You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time

Children's Privacy

Backpacker is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email.
